Detailed description of the product:

The Komatsu D355 is a heavy-duty crawler pipelayer designed to perform a full range of lifting and installation operations in the construction of oil and gas pipelines. This special vehicle is characterized by high reliability and autonomy in operation, the ability to effectively perform its tasks in complete off-road conditions - on flat or mountainous, swampy or desert areas, in a wide variety of climatic zones.

The Komatsu D355 is the most powerful and productive pipelayer in the line of pipelayers manufactured by the Japanese company Komatsu. There are also models D85C and C, which have a carrying capacity of 41 and 72 tons, respectively. All these machines are of the same type according to the schemes of their crane equipment.

The pipelayer is equipped with a Komatsu SA6D 140-2 four-stroke in-line six-cylinder diesel engine, liquid-cooled, turbocharged. The working volume of the engine is 15.24 liters. The cylinder diameter is 140 mm, the piston stroke is 165 mm. The power of this power unit is: full (SAE J1995) - 277 kW (377 horsepower), at a nominal speed of 2,000 rpm; useful - (ISO 9249 / SAE J1349) - 265 kW (361 horsepower), also at 2,000 rpm.

The Komatsu SA6D 140-2 diesel engine is equipped with diesel direct injection, a mechanical fan, an air cleaner, an alternator, a water pump, a lubricant pump, a silencer and a fuel pump, a mechanical all-mode speed controller. Lubrication is carried out by force, using a gear pump; a full-flow filter is installed in the system. The dry air cleaner with automatic dust extraction ensures a longer life of the filter element, there is a dust indicator to simplify the regular maintenance process.

Pipelayers of this model are equipped with self-contained internal combustion preheaters that heat the diesel engine, power train and battery box. The electric heater, with an external power source, is always able to keep the power unit warm.
